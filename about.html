About us

Welcome to Aluvison Farm Enterprise website

The one thing about plants and fruits is they always give benefits. Do you want to fill your home garden with lovely foliage, and soon enjoy the fruits of your labour?

Well, at Aluvison, we promote sustainable agricultural practices, strenghten markets and promote the environment in rural Africa.

We supply the best organic grafted fruit seedlings and fresh farm strawberries.

Order now

Aluvisia is a farmer / business man graduated in a degree in Agribusiness management and a diploma in Agribusiness management it was through these achievements that he was able to start his first company called Aluvison Farm enterprise. Aluvison farm enterprise has been able to supply both fruit and soft wood/hardwood seedlings to farmers across the country. We also do landscaping as well. Impacting the community We offer consulting services to farmers across the country and educate them on the importance of planting different types of tree seedings. As a company we also like giving back to the society by donating some of our resources and time to the less fortunate.
